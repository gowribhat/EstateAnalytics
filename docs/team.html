Our Story

EstateAnalytics was born from a simple observation: real estate agents were data-rich but insight-poor. While traditional property platforms focus on lead generation, we saw a need for tools that help agents communicate property value more effectively.

Our journey began at the National University of Singapore, where a team of data scientists, real estate specialists, and software engineers came together with a shared vision. Using Singapore's wealth of public property data, we built a platform that transforms complex data into actionable insights.

Today, EstateAnalytics is helping agents across Singapore close more deals by empowering them with data. And we're just getting started – our vision extends beyond Singapore to Southeast Asia and eventually global markets.

We're committed to continued innovation in real estate analytics, always focused on our core mission: helping agents communicate property value effectively to their clients.
