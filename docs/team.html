Our Story

EstateAnalytics was born out of a student project at the National University of Singapore for the course DBA3702: Descriptive Analytics with R. What started as an academic exercise evolved into a comprehensive solution addressing a real-world challenge in the real estate industry.

We observed that real estate agents were data-rich but insight-poor. While traditional property platforms focus on lead generation, we saw a need for tools that help agents communicate property value more effectively to their clients.

Leveraging Singapore's wealth of public property data, we built a platform that transforms complex data into actionable insights. Our interactive map visualizations, comprehensive building analytics, and AI-powered area assessments help agents clearly convey property value propositions.

While EstateAnalytics began as a student project, we envision it growing into an essential tool for real estate professionals across Singapore and eventually Southeast Asia. We remain committed to our core mission: helping agents communicate property value effectively to close more deals.

Academic Foundation
